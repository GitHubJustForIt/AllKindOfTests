Terms of Service

1. About this project

Phantasialand-Roblox is an independent, non-commercial fan project built inside Roblox. It has no connection to, endorsement from, or collaboration with Phantasialand, Schmidt Löffelhardt GmbH & Co. KG, or any of its affiliates.

2. Free tickets, one per username

All tickets are free. Up to two tickets can be booked per order, per day. However, each Roblox username may hold only one ticket per calendar day — a repeated booking attempt with the same username for the same day is automatically rejected.

3. Signing in with Roblox (OAuth)

If you sign in via "Continue with Roblox," this site requests only your public Roblox username through Roblox's official OAuth interface. No passwords, payment details, or other private information are ever requested or stored. You can also enter your username manually instead, without signing in.

4. Your e-ticket

After booking, you'll receive an e-ticket with a unique barcode. It can only be downloaded as a text file (.txt). Keep this file private — it serves as proof of your booking at the daily entrance session and cannot be transferred to anyone else.

5. Use & disclaimer

Use of this site is at your own risk. There is no guarantee of availability, error-free operation, or continued operation. Abuse (e.g. repeated bookings under false information) may result in exclusion from future bookings.
